Privacy Policy

Effective date: January 4, 2025

TS Tips ("us", "we", or "our") operates the TS Tips mobile application (the "Service" or "App").

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application. Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information Collection And Use

We collect several different types of information for various purposes to provide and improve our Service to you.

1.1 Types of Data Collected

Local Storage Data

When you bookmark tips in the App, this information is stored locally on your device using the device's local storage mechanism. This data includes:

  • Bookmarked Tips: List of tips you have bookmarked for quick access

Important: All bookmark data is stored exclusively on your device. We do not transmit, store, or have access to your bookmarks on external servers. This data never leaves your device.

Push Notification Data

If you enable push notifications, we may collect:

  • Device Tokens: Unique identifiers used to send push notifications to your device
  • Notification Preferences: Your preferences for receiving notifications

This data is stored securely and used only for the purpose of sending you notifications about new tips or app updates.

Usage Data

We may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve the app experience, including:

  • App version and device information
  • General usage patterns (anonymized)
  • Error reports to help fix bugs

Third-Party Content

The App fetches TypeScript tips content from a public GitHub repository. We do not collect or store this content on our servers. The content is fetched directly from GitHub's public API.

2. Use of Data

TS Tips uses the collected data for various purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the Service
  • To notify you about changes to our Service (via push notifications, if enabled)
  • To allow you to bookmark and access your favorite tips
  • To provide customer care and support
  • To provide analysis or valuable information so that we can improve the Service
  • To monitor the usage of the Service
  • To detect, prevent and address technical issues

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Local Storage

All bookmark data is stored locally on your device. This data is not synchronized with any external servers and remains on your device until you delete it or uninstall the App.

3.2 Data Security

The security of your data is important to us, but remember that no method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your data, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.
